Student Maintenance Representative (SMR)

Type: Hired by Housing and Dining

Open to: On-Campus Hanszenites

Number of positions: 1

Timeline: Apply at this link.

Description: One student in each residential college is employed to act as a liaison between the College and Housing & Dining relative to the routine physical maintenance of the college. This position serves as a representative of the Housing & Dining Department in contact with the college government. It is anticipated that the duties will require some time each day, including nights and weekends, for proper performance (approximately 10 hours a week). The individual must be a responsible on campus college resident and willing to become familiar with the physical facilities of the college. The Student Maintenance Representative’s primary responsibility is to assist the Operations Director and the Housing and Dining team in ensuring compliance with the Housing Agreement.
