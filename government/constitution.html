Bills are a way for the college to implent procedures for a time span of one (1) academic year. Resolutions convey Hanszen's official stance on a particular issue.

A passed bill is considered in effect until the end of the first cabinet of the following academic year. You can view a list of current bills below.

Any resolution in effect is listed after the bills. A resolution is considered in effect until the end of the first cabinet of the following academic year.

View the Bills and Resolutions

The Constitutional Review Committee, led by the Parliamentarians, meets every three years (most recently in Fall 2022) to review all of Hanszen's legal documents and make sure that they are up to date.
